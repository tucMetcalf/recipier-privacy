Recipi'er

Your personal AI-powered recipe manager

What is Recipi'er?

Recipi'er is a personal recipe manager that uses AI to help you capture, organise, and cook the recipes you love — wherever you find them. Import from Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest, recipe websites, photos, or plain text. Recipi'er does the hard work of extracting ingredients and steps so you can focus on cooking.

Key Features

  • 🤖AI-powered import from social media, websites, photos, and text — powered by Claude AI
  • 📱Share any recipe link directly to Recipi'er from any app
  • 📚Organise recipes into collections and share collections with family and friends
  • 🔍Search by title, ingredient, category, and dietary preference
  • 🌐Discover and save public recipes from other Recipi'er users
  • 🍳Full-screen cooking mode with step-by-step navigation and timers
  • 🛒Generate shopping lists from your meal plan automatically
  • 📅Plan your weekly meals with the built-in meal planner
  • ⚖️Scale recipes up or down and toggle between metric and imperial units
  • 💾Works offline — your recipes are always available even without internet

Built with

  • ⚡Flutter — fast, beautiful, cross-platform
  • 🗄️Supabase — secure, real-time cloud sync
  • 🧠Anthropic Claude — the AI behind recipe extraction
