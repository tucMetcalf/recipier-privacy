Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Recipi'er. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or sign in, we collect the following information:

  • Email address – used to identify and manage your account
  • Name – used to personalise your experience

We also collect recipe content that you choose to import from Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, websites, or photo scans (including handwritten recipes). This content is stored securely in your personal account.

2. How You Sign In

Recipi'er supports the following sign-in methods:

  • Email and password – you create an account directly with us
  • Sign in with Google – we receive your name and email address from Google
  • Sign in with Apple – we receive your name and email address (or Apple's private relay email) from Apple

We do not access any data from your Google or Apple accounts beyond what is needed to create and manage your Recipi'er account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Recipi'er account
  • Save and organise your imported recipes
  • Allow you to search your saved recipes
  • Provide and improve the app experience

4. Data Storage

Your recipes and account data are stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email address below and we will process your request promptly.

6. Children's Privacy

Recipi'er is suitable for all ages and has no age restrictions. We collect only the minimum information necessary (email address and name) to provide the service, regardless of the user's age. If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about your child's use of the app, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@recipier.app
